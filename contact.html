  • Shipping & Delivery Information

    We offer free shipping on all orders over $100 within the continental US. Standard shipping takes 3-5 business days, while express shipping is available for 1-2 business days delivery. International shipping is available to select countries.

    All orders are processed within 24-48 hours of payment confirmation and you'll receive a tracking number via email once your package ships.

  • Returns & Exchange Policy

    We accept returns within 30 days of purchase for unused items in original packaging with tags attached. Exchanges can be processed for different sizes or colors when available.

    To initiate a return or exchange, please contact our customer service team with your order number and reason for return.

  • Care & Maintenance Tips

    To maintain the quality of your purse, store it in the provided dust bag when not in use. Clean with a soft, dry cloth and avoid exposure to direct sunlight or rain.

    For leather items, use appropriate leather cleaner and conditioner every 3-6 months to maintain the material's quality and appearance.

  • Payment & Security

    We currently accept PayPal as our secure payment method. PayPal allows you to pay safely using your PayPal balance, bank account, or credit/debit card through their secure platform.

    All transactions are protected by PayPal's buyer protection and secured with SSL encryption to ensure your personal information remains safe.
