About the artist

Welcome to my little corner of the internet! I’m an artist, creator, and all-around craft lover with a big heart and a passion for turning everyday materials into something magical. I’ve always believed that art should feel good, not just to look at, but to create and to carry with you. That’s why I pour my soul into every piece, hoping it brings a smile to your face and a little sparkle to your day. When I’m not elbow-deep in glitter or mixing up resin, you can usually find me laughing with friends, chasing new ideas, or dreaming up my next colorful creation. Thanks for stopping by. I’m so glad you’re here!
